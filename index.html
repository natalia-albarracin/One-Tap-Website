HOW IT WORKS


OUR MISSION


Many students on Campus worry about their safety and how to reach out for help as fast as possible in an emergency. Although there are safety “Blue” buttons around Campus, some students may be in an area that does not contain one. Additionally, even though there is a strong police force presence at NJIT, especially at night, you cannot always be in view of an officer, which can leave one susceptible to danger. This app relieves our worries and allows all users to tap a single button from their mobile device and get help instantly. This will enable students and faculty to feel safe and comfortable on Campus, knowing that help is just one click away.
